About Me

My name is Taylor St Jean and I plan to major in Physics. I am finishing my Associate of Science degree in the Spring at North Seattle College and will be transferring to a four year university to continue to work towards a Master's degree in Physics.

Interests

One of my interests is breadboarding, which is a method of prototyping circuits with individual components and jumper wires. My biggest breadboarding project was a surprisingly complex 12 digit display run from an Arduino. The display is multiplexed, meaning each digit is activated one at a time and in quick enough succession to seem as if each led is lit simultaneously.



Another major interest of mine is coding, especially creating simulations using MatLab or Python. For examples of projects I have completed or am currently working on, please feel free to visit my GitHub page. This website was also entirely coded from scratch and runs from a Flask backend on a Gandi Simple Hosting server (code for this website is also on GitHub).

One of my biggest hobbies for the better part of my life has been music production. My music has evolved over the years, however I primarily write electronic dance. To hear the music I have written, feel free to visit my page on Spotify .

Aspirations

My dream is to eventually land a career at CERN in Switzerland, working as a physicist at the Large Hadron Collider. In the mean time, I have looked into teaching Physics at a university level or a career at a space agency as possible professions.
